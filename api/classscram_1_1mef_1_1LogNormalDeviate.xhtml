The log-normal deviate parametrization with its expected value and error factor of certain confidence level.

Parameters
[in]meanThe mean of the log-normal distribution not the mean of underlying normal distribution, which is parameter mu. mu is the location parameter, sigma is the scale factor. E(x) = exp(mu + sigma^2 / 2)
[in]efThe error factor of the log-normal distribution. EF = exp(z_alpha * sigma)
[in]levelThe confidence level.
